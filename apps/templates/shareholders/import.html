How it works
  1. Export your Excel sheet as .xlsx or copy into the CSV template.
  2. Columns supported: Magaca / Name, Saamiga / Shares, Lacagta / Capital, Boqoleey %.
  3. Missing emails are auto-generated (name@akramsweets.com).
  4. Rounded Excel % values are recalculated from capital so ownership totals 100%.
  5. Dashboard capital KPIs update automatically after import.
  6. Monthly Mudarabah still uses Net Profit from Odoo — capital is for the register only.
