How it works
  1. Upload Excel .xlsx or CSV with Magaca, Saamiga, Lacagta, Boqoleey %.
  2. Matching people are updated; missing people are created.
  3. Anyone not in the file is deactivated and removed from ownership totals.
  4. Murabaha / total-asset note rows become company-owned assets, not shareholders.
  5. Ownership is recalculated from capital to total 100%.
  6. Mudarabah monthly profit still uses Net Profit from Odoo only.
